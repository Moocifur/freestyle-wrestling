Our Programs

Ages 6-10

Youth Beginners

Introduction to wrestling fundamentals with emphasis on fun, basic movements, and building confidence. Perfect for young athletes just starting their wrestling journey.

  • Basic stance and movement
  • Simple takedowns
  • Mat awareness
  • Games and activities
Ages 11-13

Middle School

Intermediate training focusing on technique refinement, conditioning, and introduction to competitive wrestling concepts.

  • Advanced takedowns
  • Escapes and reversals
  • Conditioning drills
  • Tournament preparation
Ages 14-18

High School

Advanced training program designed for serious competitors looking to excel at the high school level and beyond.

  • Advanced technique work
  • Strength and conditioning
  • Mental preparation
  • College recruitment support
